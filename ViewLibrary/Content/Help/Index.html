START MENU

  • Select the menu - the up or down keys.
  • Open menu item - the enter key.

GAME SCREN

  • Moving the player's tank - the up, down, left and right keys.
  • Shooting the player's tank - the key space.
  • Pause the game - the key p.

CONSTRUCTION

  • Moving the player's tank - the up, down, left and right keys.
  • To change a material of the designer - the key enter.
  • Add material to the map - the key space.
  • Exit and save the map - the key s.

ALL SCREN

  • Close the game - the key esc.
  • Help - the key f1.
  • Volume sound - PgUp or PgDown keys.
